Today's topic entities

what is entities

Entities are objects that have a distinct existence in the context of a system or application. They can represent real-world objects, concepts, or data structures.

  • Entities can be physical objects like a car, a person, or a product.
  • They can also be abstract concepts like a transaction, an event, or a user session.
why we use entities

Entities are used to model and represent the data and behavior of objects in a system. They help in organizing and structuring data, making it easier to manage and manipulate.

  • Entities provide a way to encapsulate data and behavior related to a specific object.
  • They allow for better organization of code and data, making it easier to understand and maintain.
symbols

  • Physical Objects: Icons representing cars, houses, or other tangible items.
  • Abstract Concepts: Symbols like gears for processes, arrows for flow, or circles for events.
